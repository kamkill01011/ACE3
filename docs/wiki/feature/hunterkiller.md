## 1. Overview
Allows a gunner and commander to observe and remote control the other's weapon to their own target

## 2. Usage

### 2.1 Observe other turret
- Press <kbd>Q</kbd>

### 2.2 Override other turret
- Place the crosshair on the enemy vehicle.
- Press <kbd>E</kbd>
